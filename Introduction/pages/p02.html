By the end of this course, you will:
  • Be able to describe the key concepts and obligations of the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and how they are applied to NTU student organisations (e.g. societies and clubs)
  • Be aware of your responsibilities when collecting, using and disclosing personal data, especially in regard to obtaining consent from the individual and protecting his/her personal data securely
  • Be aware that both your student organisation and you, personally, may be held criminally liable for breaching the PDPA
