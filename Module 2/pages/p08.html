In this module, you have learnt:

  1. The Do Not Call (DNC) provisions is a set of provisions under the PDPA which regulates the use of Singapore telephone numbers by organisations for marketing purposes.
  2. All student organisations must obtain clear and unambiguous consent in written form from the individual prior to sending him/her a message for marketing purposes.
  3. When sending a specified message, the organisation must include clear and accurate information:
    • that identifies the organisation sending the message;
    • about how the recipient can contact the organisation.
