Transfer of Personal Data Overseas

Be aware that transferring personal data out of Singapore is not limited to just the physical transfer of the personal data to the intended recipient.

Click on each picture to see examples of situations in which personal data can be transferred overseas.

Sending emails/letters/fax messages containing personal data overseas
Passing the recipient physical storage media such as thumb drives and CD, which contain personal data
Verbally providing personal data to a recipient overseas through a phone call, including leaving a voice message
