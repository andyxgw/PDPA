Transfer Limitation

Organisations that transfer personal data out of Singapore must take steps to ensure that the data transferred is provided a standard of protection that is comparable to the protection under the PDPA.

Click on each box to learn what organisations must do before transferring any personal data overseas.

Ensure that the overseas recipient of the personal data is bound by legally enforceable obligations to provide to transferred personal data a standard of protection that is comparable to that under the PDPA.
Obtain assurance that the recipient has appropriate security policies and measures that safeguard the personal data in accordance with the PDPA.
Obtain assurance that personal data being transferred will only be used in accordance with the PDPA.
