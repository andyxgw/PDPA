Deemed Consent

These are instances where consent is deemed to have been given:

  • An individual provides his/her personal data voluntarily.
  • The individual was aware of the purposes for which the personal data was provided.
  • The circumstances are such that it is reasonable for the individual to have provided his/her personal data.
The onus is on the organisation to show that the individual is aware of the purposes for which his/her personal data is being collected.
