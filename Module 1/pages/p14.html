Obtaining Personal Data from Third Parties
 
An organisation should exercise due diligence when collecting personal data from third party sources (i.e., not directly from the individual himself).
 
The organisation must obtain either:
 
  1. written assurance from the third party that it has obtained the consent of the individual to disclose the personal data; or
    2.  
  2. a copy of the document evidencing the consent given by the individuals concerned to the third party to disclose the personal data.
